Our Vision

"To raise Christians who live according to God's Perfect Will for their lives instead of their own Will or God's permissive Will."

Understanding God's Will

Perfect Will

God's ideal plan for our lives, bringing maximum fulfillment and impact.

Permissive Will

What God allows when we choose our own path, often with less blessing.

Our Will

Human choices made without seeking divine direction.

Our Mission

"To broaden the Christians' relationship with Jesus as their Lord and Master in order to enable them fulfill their God's purposes through proclamation of the word of God, Divine teachings, prophecy, prayer, genuine intimacy of the Holy Spirit."

Word of God

Systematic teaching of Scripture to build biblical foundations.

Prayer & Prophecy

Developing a vibrant prayer life and sensitivity to God's voice.

Holy Spirit

Cultivating genuine intimacy with the Holy Spirit for empowered living.

Discipleship

Practical training for serving Christ in everyday life.
